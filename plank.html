3.) plank.

Plank is a powerful bodyweight exercise that targets the core muscles, including the abs, back, and shoulders. The body is held in a straight position, supported by the forearms and toes. Planks improve core stability, posture, and overall strength. This exercise also helps reduce back pain and improve balance. It is simple in appearance but very effective.
