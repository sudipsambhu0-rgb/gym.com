9.) Leg raises

Leg Raises are a core-strengthening exercise that mainly targets the lower abdominal muscles. The movement involves lifting the legs upward while lying flat on the back. This exercise improves core stability, strengthens the abs, and enhances body control. Leg Raises also help improve hip flexibility. They are commonly used in fitness routines focused on abdominal strength and endurance.
