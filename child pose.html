11.) Child pose

Child Pose is a gentle stretching exercise commonly used in yoga and recovery routines. In this position, the body is relaxed while stretching the lower back, hips, thighs, and shoulders. It helps reduce stress, calm the mind, and improve flexibility. Child Pose is often performed after intense workouts to relax the muscles and normalize breathing. It is ideal for beginners, older adults, and anyone needing recovery or relaxation.
